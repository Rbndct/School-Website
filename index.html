What sets iACADEMY apart

Project Based Learning

At iAcademy, we believe in learning by doing. Our project-based approach allows students to apply lessons to real-world scenarios, ensuring that knowledge is not only gained but retained.

High-tech Facilities & Equipment

Experience world-class education at iACADEMY - the Philippines' first IBM Center of Excellence and WACOM Authorized Training Partner.

Learn 21st Century Skills

At iACADEMY, we go beyond traditional education by prioritizing the development of essential skills such as critical thinking, creativity, communication, leadership, and collaboration. Our students are equipped with the necessary tools to excel in a competitive global landscape.
