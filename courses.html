SHS TRACKS

In the Philippines, the Senior High School (SHS) program offers different academic tracks that students can choose from based on their interests and career goals. These academic tracks are designed to prepare students for college, employment, or entrepreneurship.

Why go through Senior High School

Preparation for Higher Education

Unlock your potential with senior high school education. Develop critical thinking skills, explore interests and passions through elective courses, and pave the way for a successful career.

Improved Career Opportunities

Unlock a world of possibilities with senior high school education. Develop discipline, perseverance, and intellectual capacity to impress potential employers and open doors to higher-paying careers.

Personal Growth & Development

Senior high school ignites personal growth through life skills, diverse perspectives, and socialization. Graduates emerge responsible, well-rounded and primed to make a positive impact in society.
