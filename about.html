iACADEMY THROUGH THE YEARS

iACADEMY's history since then

2002

iACADEMY: Reinventing Education Since 2002

iACADEMY was founded in 2002 with the goal of reinventing education.

2009

iACADEMY: WACOM & IBM Partner in PH

iACADEMY was named the first WACOM authorized training partner in the Philippines in 2009. It was also designated as the ASEAN region's first IBM Center of Excellence.

2010

iACADEMY Partners with Project Runway PH

In 2010, iACADEMY collaborated with Seasons 3 and 4 of Project Runway Philippines.

2011

iACADEMY & Weather PH Launch Weather App

In 2011, iACADEMY collaborated with Weather Philippines to create a website and mobile app for real-time weather updates.

2015

iACADEMY's Battle League: First 24-Hour Game Competition

iACADEMY hosted the first 24-hour game competition, Battle League, in 2015.

2016

iACADEMY Launches Specialized SHS Programs

iACADEMY began offering specialized Senior High School programs in 2016.

2017

iACADEMY Partners with UNITY & IGDA for Game Development Training & Certification

As a Certified Training and Certification Partner, iACADEMY began providing training and certification with UNITY in 2017. It also became the International Game Developers Association's official partner in hosting Manila Game Jam 2017 as part of the Global Game Jam in January 2017.

2018

iACADEMY Named First AWS Educational Partner in PH for Cloud Computing Certifications

Amazon Web Services (AWS) chose iACADEMY as the first and only school in the Philippines to offer Cloud Computing Certification Courses in 2018. Amazon Web Services (AWS) has named iACADEMY its first educational partner.

2019

iACADEMY's 2019 Partnerships and Collaborations: PwC, Toon Boom, PyCon APAC, DOT, MusKKat, Unilab, & CCC.

iACADEMY had several partnerships and collaborations in 2019. In addition to hosting PyCon APAC 2019 with PythonPH, the school created a mobile app for travel in the Philippines, augmented reality promotional materials, and "Maze," a social distancing app. iACADEMY also collaborated with Museo ng Kaalam√°ng Katutub (MusKKat), Unilab, and the Climate Change Commission (CCC).

2020

iACADEMY: First to Implement Alibaba Global Digital Talent Program in the Philippines

In 2020, iACADEMY was named the Philippines' first educational institution to implement the Alibaba Global Digital Talent program.

2021

iACADEMY Receives Multiple International Awards in 2021 & Establishes Partnerships while Launching a New Program.

Global Brands named iACADEMY the Most Innovative Education Provider in 2021, joining other prestigious institutions such as Harvard and MIT. It was also acknowledged by publications such as Global Business Review Magazine, International Business Magazine, World Economic Magazine, and World Business Outlook. In the same year, iACADEMY partnered with Singapore's Temasek Polytechnic to launch its BA Music Production and Sound Design program in collaboration with Avid and Dolby Atmos.

2022

iACADEMY's 2022 Achievements: Awards & Top Rankings

iACADEMY successfully collaborated with ASUS Edukasyon and the School of Business and Liberal Arts in 2022. Global Business Review Magazine also named them Best Design School, Best School for Music Production, Design, and Technology, Best Education Provider, and Most Innovative Education Provider (UAE). Furthermore, iACADEMY was named Leading Arts and Design Education Provider and Most Innovative Education Provider in the Philippines 2022 by World Business Outlook Magazine in Singapore, and it ranked first in the Real Estate Appraiser Licensure Exam with a 93.75% passing rate.

About iACADEMY

The Information and Communications Technology Academy, also known as iAcademy (stylized as iACADEMY) in the Philippines, is a private, non-sectarian educational institution.

In iACADEMY, four different tracks are available:

  • The first is the Academic Track, which has two strands: Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM) and Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS).
  • The second one is the Technical Vocational (Tech-Voc) Track, wherein we offer 4 strands: Software Development, Animation, Fashion Design, and Graphic Illustration.
  • Last is the Arts and Design Track, in which we offer one strand in Multimedia Arts.
