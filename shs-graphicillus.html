GRAPHIC ILLUSTRATION

Strand Description

The Graphic Illustration (GI) Strand provides students with broadened, practical and hands-on understanding of the field. Skills and knowledge in traditional and digital drawing, painting are enhanced in order to apply to comic arts, book illustrations, film, animation or storyboard-making.

GI focuses on areas such as 2D and 3D methodology, motion graphics, production art and creative writing.
